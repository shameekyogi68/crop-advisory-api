# Udupi District Soil Micro-Zonation Master Document

## 1. Taluk: UDUPI
**Base Condition:** Lateritic, Acidic, Low N, High P, Medium K.

### Zone A: Coastal Strip (Malpe/Kaup Coast)
*   **Geography:** West of Longitude 74.70°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy / Sandy Loam
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Drainage: Exacting/Excessive.
    *   Nutrients: Low Potassium (Leaching), Low Sulphur.
    *   pH: Neutral (6.5-7.5). Seasonal: Alkaline in Pre-Monsoon.
*   **Crops:** Coconut, Mogra (Jasmine).

### Zone B: Hinterland (Manipal/Parkala)
*   **Geography:** East of 74.70°E (Main Taluk Area).
*   **Soil Type:** Lateritic
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Drainage: Well Drained.
    *   Nutrients: Medium Potassium. High Phosphorus (Accumulated).
    *   pH: Acidic (5.0-5.8).
*   **Crops:** Paddy, Arecanut.

## 2. Taluk: KUNDAPURA
**Base Condition:** Sandy Loam, Acidic, Low N, Medium P, Low K.

### Zone A: Estuarine Delta (Gangolli/Tallur)
*   **Geography:** West of 74.68°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy / Alluvial
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Salinity: HIGH (March-May).
    *   pH: Neutral to Alkaline.
    *   Nutrients: Deficient in Zn, B.
*   **Crops:** Coconut, Paddy (Saline-tolerant).

### Zone B: Uplands (Siddapura/Towards Ghats)
*   **Geography:** East of 74.75°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Lateritic / Gravelly
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Texture: Course, Rocky.
    *   Nutrients: Low Fertility, Low Water Holding.
*   **Crops:** Cashew, Rubber.

## 3. Taluk: HEBRI
**Base Condition:** Clay Loam, Slightly Acidic, Low P.

### Zone A: Forest Fringe (Foot of Ghats)
*   **Geography:** Entire Taluk (East of 74.90°E).
*   **Soil Type:** Clay Loam
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Organic Matter: High (Forest litter).
    *   CEC: High (Better nutrient retention).
    *   pH: 5.5 - 6.0 (Buffered).
    *   Nutrients: Medium K, Low P.

## 4. Taluk: BRAHMAVAR
**Base Condition:** Sandy Clay Loam, Acidic, Low K.

### Zone A: River Basin (Swarna/Sita Rivers)
*   **Geography:** Lowlands (Lat: 13.40-13.45, Lon: 74.70-74.75).
*   **Soil Type:** Alluvial / Silty Clay
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Moisture: High retention.
    *   Nutrients: Enriched by river deposits.
*   **Crops:** Intensive Paddy.

### Zone B: General Plains
*   **Geography:** Remainder of Taluk.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy Clay Loam
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   pH: Acidic.
    *   Nutrients: Low Potassium (Generalized).

## 5. Taluk: BYNDOOR
**Base Condition:** Lateritic, Low K.

### Zone A: Coastal Belt (Maravanthe)
*   **Geography:** Longitude < 74.65°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Erosion: Severe (Sea erosion).
    *   Salinity: High risk.
    *   Nutrients: Very Low K, Low N.

### Zone B: Interior (Baindur Town/Inland)
*   **Geography:** Longitude > 74.65°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Lateritic
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Crust: Hard Iron-rich crust common.
*   **Crops:** Cashew, Mango.

## 6. Taluk: KARKALA
**Base Condition:** Lateritic, Acidic, Low K.

### Zone A: Rocky Plateau
*   **Geography:** Most of Taluk boundaries.
*   **Soil Type:** Lateritic (Gravelly)
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Depth: Shallow in places.
    *   Acidity: Strong (High rainfall leaching).
    *   Nutrients: Low P fixing capacity is high.

## 7. Taluk: KAPU
**Base Condition:** Sandy Clay Loam, High P.

### Zone A: Coastal (Kaup Beach Area)
*   **Geography:** West of 74.72°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   pH: Neutral.
    *   Nutrients: Low Organic Carbon.

### Zone B: Inland (Polipu/Majur)
*   **Geography:** East of 74.72°E.
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy Clay Loam
*   **Characteristics:**
    *   Nutrients: High P (Horticulture belt).
    *   Moisture: Good retention.
